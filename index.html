Welcome to HPCL Rajasthan Refinery Limited

HPCL Rajasthan Refinery Ltd. (HRRL) was incorporated on 18th September, 2013, as a Joint Venture between Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited (HPCL) and Government of Rajasthan (GOR) with an equity participation of 74% and 26% respectively.

MAJOR FEATURES OF COMPLEX:
  • HRRL is a joint Venture (JV) of HPCL (74%) and Govt. of Rajasthan (26%), setting up a 9 Million Metric Tonnes per Annum (MMTPA) grass root Refinery cum Petrochemical Complex.
  • It is the first Grass-root Integrated Refinery cum Petrochemical Complex of India.
  • It is designed to process a blend of Rajasthan crude and other imported crudes.
  • It is designed to have state-of-the art world class technologies to make environmental friendly BS VI fuels and high value petrochemicals products.
  • Environmental Clearance for HRRL was granted by Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change on September 13, 2017.
  • Govt. of India approval for the project was received on October 9, 2017.
  • To receive Crude and water seamlessly, a robust network of 800 Km cross country pipeline is being made.
  • To construct this mega refinery, more than 150 lakhs m3 of earth filling has been done, which is more than six times the volume of the Pyramid of Giza.
  • Approximately 16 lakh m3 of concrete is used which is 5 times more than the concrete used in Burj Khalifa.
  • About 3 lakhs metric tons of structural steel is used, a quantity 40 times more than the amount used in the construction of the Eiffel Tower.
  • Approximately 28,000 Km of electrical and instrumentation cables are being installed, surpassing twice the Earth's diameter.
  • Refinery Main Control Room / Petrochemical Main Control Room (RMCR/PMCR) are one of the largest in the country.
  • Employment Generation for 35,000 people during peak construction.
  • Indirect Employment for about 1,00,000 peoples for support infrastructure.
  • In the construction of this refinery, 85% of the components are sourced locally, aligning with India's focus on the Atmanirbhar Bharat initiative.
